## Typography

- **Display / headings:** `'Inter', -apple-system, system-ui, sans-serif` — weight 600–700
- **Body:** `'Inter', -apple-system, system-ui, sans-serif` — weight 400
- **Scale:** 14 · 16 · 18 · 20 · 24 · 32 · 40 · 56 · 72

## Layout Posture

- Sharp, confident geometry (4px radii on UI, 0–8px on cards)
- Navy as dominant background in hero/footers; white surfaces for content
- Yellow used sparingly as a light accent (never as text on navy — contrast fails)
- Red reserved for primary CTAs only, used at most once per screen
- Generous whitespace, content-top-biased, no centered vertical stacks
